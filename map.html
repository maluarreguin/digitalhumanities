Maps

Unfortunately, maps were not my strong suit in this project whatsoever, and while my original task was to highlight each accused person whose case pertains to fairires manually from the database, I was unable to do so. I believe I was only able to get through about twenty entries before Tableau decided to become more difficult than I could manage and would not dispaly new records that I was attempting to incorporate into the map. While I do not know why this happened, I am left with some data, which is what I have here today. Additionally, I could not figure out ArcGIS either. Regardless, the conclusion I will draw from what I was able to create is essentially that there is no inherent correlation regarding locations of the accused whose cases pertain to fairies. If anything, I would argue that the lore of fairies that existed at this time exceeds distances or locations, and is rather a small and or natural part of the greater dialogue of Scottish folklore in this era.Â
