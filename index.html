The Presence of Fairies in Scottish Witchcraft

This website is the culmination of a quarter long project completed for Miriam Posner's Digital Humaities 201 course at UCLA for Winter Quarter 2023. It is in part an effort to achieve the Digital Humanities Graduate Certificate at UCLA. On this website you will find my final project argument, the methods that I took to approach this topic, as well as the data visualizations and maps necessary to complete this project, including my piece's bibliography. My hope is for this page to be a useful source of information pertaining to the historical context of the presence of fairies in Scottish witchcraft and to the front of the digital humanities at large.
