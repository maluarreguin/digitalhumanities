WHAT IS A WITCH?

"Witchcraft traditionally means the use of magic or supernatural powers to harm others. A practitioner is a witch. The word witch derives from the Old English nouns wiċċa [ˈwit.t͡ʃɑ] ('male witch, warlock') and wiċċe [ˈwit.t͡ʃe] ('female witch'). The word's further origins in Proto-Germanic and Proto-Indo-European are unclear." (Internet, Wikipedia)
